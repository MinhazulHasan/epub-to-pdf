REAR WINDOW AND OUTSIDE MIRROR DEFOGGER BUTTON

[DEFOGGER SWITCH, HEATED REAR WINDOW, REAR WINDOW HEATING, REAR WINDOW HEATER]

Touch the rear window defogger switch to defog the rear window glass and outside mirrors.

The indicator light on the button comes on.

Touch the button again to turn the defogger off.

The rear window defogger automatically turns off after approximately 20 minutes.
