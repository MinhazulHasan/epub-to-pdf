STEERING WHEEL ADJUSTMENT

[GETTING STARTED, #F18626, #FDDDC0]

Manual Steering Wheel Adjustment

Pull the lock lever down and adjust the steering wheel up, down, forward or rearward to the desired position. Push the lock lever up securely to lock the steering wheel in place.

Electric Steering Wheel Adjustment

Move the lever to adjust the steering wheel up or down, forward or backward until the desired position. The memory seat system will keep the setting.
