HEAD-UP DISPLAY

[GETTING STARTED, #F18626, #FDDDC0]

The Head-Up Display (HUD) can display one or more of the following features:

1

Navigation / Audio / SMS information

2

Current speed

3

Last recognised traffic sign

4

Cruise control information

Activation

Press the Head-Up Display button to toggle On/Off.

TIP:

If the HUD is turned off, it will remain off even if the vehicle is restarted.

Use the vehicle information display to adjust the settings of the HUD to suit you.

[Settings] -> [Head-Up Display].
