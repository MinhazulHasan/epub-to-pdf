Electric Power Steering warning light

[STEERING WHEEL, EXCLAMATION MARK, EPS]

Illuminates when the power switch is placed in the ON position, turns off when the power switch is placed in the READY to drive position.

If the light illuminates at any other time, it may indicate that the Electric Power Steering system is not functioning properly.

Contact a NISSAN certified electric vehicle dealer for support.

If an Electric Power Steering malfunction occurs, the power assist function is limited or turned off. The steering operates normally, but requires greater force than usual.
