ProPILOT Assist

[DRIVING AIDS, #1CACE4, #BCE4FA]

The ProPILOT Assist system is intended to enhance the operation of the vehicle when following a vehicle travelling in the same lane and direction with:

  • Intelligent Cruise Control (ICC): Helps manage speed and distance from the vehicle in front above 30 km/h (20 MPH).
  • Traffic Jam Pilot: Helps manage speed and distance from the vehicle in front below 30 km/h (20 MPH) and while stopped in traffic.
  • Steering Assist: Helps keep the vehicle within the travelling lane.
  • Speed Limit link: Helps adjust set speed with new speed limit.
  • Cruise Navi link: Helps adjust vehicle speed for corners, junctions and roundabouts.

  • ProPILOT Assist will not drive the car for you.
  • Be ready to drive without assistance at all times.
  • You, the driver, remain in control while using ProPILOT Assist.

ProPILOT Assist is an advanced driver assist system. The Owner’s Manual contains more detailed information to help you operate the system safely.

It is strongly recommended that you fully read this guide, and consult the information in the Owner’s Manual before using ProPILOT Assist.

Activating ProPILOT Assist

1

Press the ProPILOT Assist Switch, the following icons will be displayed.

2

Accelerate to your desired cruising speed and press down the SET- button.

TIP:
  • To overtake the vehicle in front, turn on the turn indicator. The vehicle will accelerate up to the set speed and Steering Assist will be deactivated.
  • When your vehicle has stopped for more than 3 seconds, press the accelerator pedal or the Res+ switch to drive away.
