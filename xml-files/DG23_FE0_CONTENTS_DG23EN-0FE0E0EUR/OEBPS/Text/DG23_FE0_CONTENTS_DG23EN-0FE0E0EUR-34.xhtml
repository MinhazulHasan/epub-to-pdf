VOICE RECOGNITION

[INFOTAINMENT & MULTIMEDIA, #8A78B6, #DBD5EC]

The Voice Recognition button on the steering wheel allows for hands free operation of your NissanConnect system.

Siri/ Talk to Google

Press and hold the button to activate Siri/Talk to Google. A compatible phone must be connected.

Inbuilt Voice Recognition

Short press the button or say "Hello Nissan" to activate the inbuilt voice recognition.
