PHONE BUTTON

[PHONE CALL, ACCEPT CALL]

Push to answer a received call, to end an active call or to read a received text message. Push and hold to reject an incoming call or to ignore a received text message.

When no phone call is received or when there is no active phone call, pushing this switch will display the phone screen.

Depending on models and conditions, pushing and holding this switch will start a voice recognition session for making a phone call.
