Powered Tailgate

[GETTING STARTED, #F18626, #FDDDC0]

Motion Activation

With the Intelligent Key in range, use a single quick forward and backward continuous kick motion under the centre of the rear bumper to open the powered tailgate. Do not pause at the top of the kick.

Note:

Use a second quick kick motion under the centre of the rear bumper to stop the powered tailgate from opening.
