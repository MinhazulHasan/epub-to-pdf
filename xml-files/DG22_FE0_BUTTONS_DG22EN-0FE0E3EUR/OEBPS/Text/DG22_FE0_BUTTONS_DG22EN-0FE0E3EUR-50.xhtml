SUNSHADE SWITCH

[SUNROOF, SUNSHADE SWITCH, GLASS ROOF]

When the sunshade switch is pushed rearward to the OPEN position, the sunshade opens.

When the sunshade switch is pushed forward to the CLOSE position, the sunshade will automatically close.

To stop the sunshade during opening/closing, push the sunshade switch in either direction.

NOTE:

If the sunshade is closed, operating the sunroof causes the sunshade to open along with the sunroof.
