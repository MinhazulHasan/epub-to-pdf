The Vehicle Information Display is a customisable full screen display that:

  • Keeps you informed with key vehicle information, such as speed, battery charge level and vehicle driving range
  • Shows the information that is important to you, such as navigation, energy recovery or music
  • Allows you to customise your ARIYA Vehicle Settings, including driving aids, language and display options

Vehicle Information Display Operation

You can use the Controls on the Steering Wheel as shown to control and navigate around the vehicle information display.
