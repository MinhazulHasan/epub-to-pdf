ANDROID AUTO

[INFOTAINMENT & MULTIMEDIA, #8A78B6, #DBD5EC]

With Android Auto, your in-vehicle system can be used as a display and controller for some of the Android smartphone functions.

1

Touch [] to return to the NissanConnect Home Menu

2

Touch [] to return to the Android Auto Home Menu

Connect a compatible Android smartphone with a certified cable to the USB port in the centre console of the vehicle.

TIP:

To activate the Google Voice Recognition feature, press and hold the Voice Recognition button on the right side of the steering wheel.

For additional information on the Android Auto system, please refer to the separately provided NissanConnect Owner's Manual.

Note:
  • If Android does not start up automatically confirm the Android Auto settings in the [Connections] menu, navigate to [Bluetooth] and touch the [] next to the device you would like to pair. Next ensure the settings for [Start-up Options] is set to [Auto] and the setting for [Android Auto] is set to [Active].

  • Apps and languages shown on the display will vary according to your phone settings.
  • Availability of Android Auto and its features may vary depending on your country / region.
