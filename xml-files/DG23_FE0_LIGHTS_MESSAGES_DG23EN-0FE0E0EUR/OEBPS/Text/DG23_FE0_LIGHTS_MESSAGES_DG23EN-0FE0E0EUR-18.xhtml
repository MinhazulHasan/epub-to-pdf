Slip indicator light

[SLIP, SKID, ABS, ESP, DRIVING AIDS]

Illuminates briefly when the power switch is placed in the ON or READY to drive position.

Blinks when the Electronic Stability Programme (ESP) system or the traction control system operates, indicating road surface may be slippery.
