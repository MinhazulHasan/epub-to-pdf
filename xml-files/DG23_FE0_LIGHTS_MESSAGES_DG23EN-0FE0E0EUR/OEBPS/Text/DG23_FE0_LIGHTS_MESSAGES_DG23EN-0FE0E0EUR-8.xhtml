Supplemental air bag warning light

[AIR BAG, SEAT BELT, SRS]

Illuminates briefly when the power switch is placed in the ON or READY to drive position.

If any of the following conditions occur, the front air bag, side air bag, curtain air bag and/or pretensioner systems need servicing.

  • The light re-mains on after approximately 7 seconds.
  • The light flashes intermittently.
  • The light does not illuminate at all.

Contact a NISSAN certified electric vehicle dealer for support.
