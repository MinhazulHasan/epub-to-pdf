INTELLIGENT CRUISE CONTROL

[DRIVING AIDS, #1CACE4, #BCE4FA]

The ICC system is intended to enhance the operation of the vehicle when following a vehicle travelling in the same lane and direction with:

  • Intelligent Cruise Control (ICC): Helps manage speed and distance from the vehicle in front above 30 km/h (20 MPH).
  • Speed Limit link: helps adjust set speed with new speed limit.

  • ICC will not drive the car for you.
  • Be ready to drive without assistance at all times.
  • You, the driver, remain in control while using ICC.

ICC is an advanced driver assist system. The Owner’s Manual contains more detailed information to help you operate the system safely.

It is strongly recommended that you fully read this guide, and consult the information in the Owner’s Manual before using ICC.

Activating Intelligent Cruise Control

1

Press the ICC Switch, the following icons will be displayed.

2

Accelerate to your desired cruising speed and press down the SET- button.

TIP:
  • For vehicles with navigation system: to overtake the vehicle in front, turn on the turn indicator. The vehicle will accelerate up to the set speed.
  • The ICC will cancel when the vehicle judges it has come to a standstill. Apply the brakes as necessary.
