Electronic Parking Brake warning light

[BRAKES, PARKING BRAKE, PARKING]

The warning light indicates that the electronic parking brake is applied.

Illuminates when the power switch is placed in the ON position.

Turns off when the power switch is placed in the READY TO DRIVE position and the parking brake is released.

Ensure the warning light has turned off before driving.

If the electronic parking brake warning light illuminates or flashes while the brake system warning light is illuminated, it may indicate that the Electronic Parking Brake system is not functioning properly.

Contact a NISSAN certified electric vehicle dealer for support.
