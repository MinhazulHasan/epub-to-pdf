SHIFT LEVER & OPERATION (P, D, N AND B)

[GETTING STARTED, #F18626, #FDDDC0]

1

Press and hold to unlock reverse gear

2

Press to shift into Park position

3

Home position (central position)

Subsequent shifting to D/Ds will switch between Drive and Drive Sport modes.

Shift to the P (Park), R (Reverse) or D (Drive) position only when the vehicle is completely stopped and your foot is on the brake pedal.
