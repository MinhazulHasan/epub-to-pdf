USING A MODE-3 CABLE

[CHARGING, #66A84B, #CDDDBE]

The Mode-3 cable is meant to be used at domestic charging stations without an attached cable, and at public charging stations.

It provides 16-32 amperes AC power (6.6 kW max) to charge the battery.

Outlet plug

Mode-3: Insert the plug into a Mode-3 outlet on a domestic charging station or on a public charging station
