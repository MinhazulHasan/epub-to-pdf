HEADLIGHT AIMING CONTROL

[HEADLIGHT HEIGHT ADJUSTMENT, ADJUSTING HEADLIGHTS]

Depending on the number of occupants in the vehicle and the load it is carrying, the headlight axis may be higher than desired. If the vehicle is travelling on a hilly road, the headlights may directly hit the Rear-view mirror of the vehicle ahead or the windscreen of an oncoming vehicle.

The light axis can be lowered using the switch located on the driverâ€™s side of the instrument panel, next to the steering wheel.

The higher the number designated on the switch, the lower the axis.

When travelling with no heavy load on a flat road, select position 0.

NISSAN recommends consulting local regulations on the use of lights.
