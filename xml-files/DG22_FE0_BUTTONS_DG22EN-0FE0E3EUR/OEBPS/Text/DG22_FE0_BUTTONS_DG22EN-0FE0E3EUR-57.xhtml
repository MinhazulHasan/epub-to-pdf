DOOR MIRROR ADJUSTMENT

[EXTERIOR DOOR MIRRORS, REAR VIEW MIRROR]

Manual folding

The outside rear-view mirrors fold when the outside rear-view mirror folding button is pushed.

To unfold, push the button again.

Mirror Adjustment

The outside mirrors can be adjusted by pushing the left or right mirror switch on the side you wish to adjust.

Once a side is selected, the adjustment knob can be used to adjust the position.
