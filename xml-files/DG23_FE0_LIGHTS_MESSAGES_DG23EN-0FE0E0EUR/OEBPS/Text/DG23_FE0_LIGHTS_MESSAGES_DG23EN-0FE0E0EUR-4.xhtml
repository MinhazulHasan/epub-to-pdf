Electric Vehicle system warning light

[EV system, Electric Vehicle system]

Illuminates briefly when the power switch is placed in the ON or READY to drive position.

This light illuminates if the Emergency shut off system is activated, or a malfunction in any of the following systems occurs:

  • Traction motor and inverter system.
  • Charge port or on-board charger.
  • Li-ion battery system.
  • Cooling system.
  • Electric Shift Control system.
  • Emergency shut off system is activated.

Contact a NISSAN certified electric vehicle dealer for support.
