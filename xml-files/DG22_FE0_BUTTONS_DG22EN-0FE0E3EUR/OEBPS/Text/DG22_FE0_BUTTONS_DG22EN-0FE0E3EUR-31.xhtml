AIR RECIRCULATION BUTTON

[INSIDE AIR, INTERIOR AIR, AIR RECIRCULATION SWITCH]

Touch the air recirculation button to recirculate interior air inside the vehicle.

The indicator on the center display will come on.

Air recirculation cannot be activated when the air conditioner is in front defogging mode.

When the temperature outside exceeds 21°C (70°F), the air conditioning system may default to air recirculation mode automatically to reduce overall power consumption.
