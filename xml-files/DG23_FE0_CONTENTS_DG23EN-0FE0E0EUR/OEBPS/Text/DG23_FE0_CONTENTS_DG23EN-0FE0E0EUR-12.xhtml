POWERED TAILGATE RELEASE BUTTON

[GETTING STARTED, #F18626, #FDDDC0]

Manual Opening and Closing

Push the button located either on the Instrument Panel or Intelligent Key for more than 1 second to open the powered tailgate automatically.

Push any of the operation buttons to close automatically.

Close and Lock

Push the lock switch on the powered tailgate or on the key for more than 1 second to close and lock the powered tailgate automatically.

Note:

To open the powered tailgate, the vehicle must be in the P (Park) position.
