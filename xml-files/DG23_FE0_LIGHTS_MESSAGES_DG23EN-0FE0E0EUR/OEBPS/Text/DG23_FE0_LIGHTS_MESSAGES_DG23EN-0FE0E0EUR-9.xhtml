Anti-lock Braking System (ABS) warning light

[BRAKES, ABS]

Illuminates briefly when the power switch is placed in the ON or READY to drive position.

If the ABS warning light illuminates while driving, it may indicate the ABS is not functioning properly. Contact a NISSAN certified electric vehicle dealer for support.

If an ABS malfunction occurs, the anti-lock function is turned off. The brake system operates normally, but without anti-lock assistance.
