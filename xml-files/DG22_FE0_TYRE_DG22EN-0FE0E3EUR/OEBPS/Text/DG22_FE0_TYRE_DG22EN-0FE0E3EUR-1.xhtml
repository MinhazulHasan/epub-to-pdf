TEMPORARY-USE SPARE TYRE (WHERE FITTED)

The temporary-use spare tyre is designed for emergency use only. This spare tyre should be used ONLY for very short periods and NEVER be used for long drives or extended periods.

Observe the following precautions if the temporary use spare tyre must be used, otherwise your vehicle could be damaged or involved in an accident.

WARNING

Any continuous road use of this tyre could result in tyre failure, loss of vehicle control, and possible personal injury.

CAUTION

  • The temporary-use spare tyre should be used only in emergencies. It should be replaced by the standard tyre at the first opportunity.
  • Drive carefully and do not drive your vehicle faster than 80 km/h (50 MPH).
  • Avoid driving over obstacles.
  • Avoid driving sharp turns and abrupt braking.
  • Do not exceed the vehicleâ€™s maximum load rating or the load-carrying capacity molded on the sidewall of the temporary-use spare tyre.
  • Do not use a snow chain on the temporary-use spare tyre because it will not fit properly. This could cause damage to the vehicle and result in loss of the chain.
  • Do not use the temporary-use spare tyre on any other vehicle because this tyre has been designed specifically for your vehicle.
  • The vehicle must not be driven with more than one temporary-use spare tyre at the same time.
  • Do not tow a trailer.
  • As with all tyres, the temporary-use spare tyre must be checked regularly to ensure pressure is maintained.
  • Do not take the vehicle through an automatic car wash.
