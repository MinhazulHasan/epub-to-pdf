Seat belt warning light

[SEAT BELT, SRS]

Illuminates whenever the power switch is in the ON position and any occupant’s seat belt is not fastened.

When vehicle speed exceeds 15 km/h (10 MPH) the warning light flashes and a chime will sound for at least 95 seconds or until all occupant’s seat belts are securely fastened.

The system will reset when either rear door is opened while the vehicle is stationary.
