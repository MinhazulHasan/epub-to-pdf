1.

Touch [Vehicle Hotspot] to turn on the vehicle hotspot.

2.

Scan the QR code or follow the on screen instructions to pair your device to your vehicle's Wi-Fi hotspot.

Note:

Wi-Fi Hotspot is available for an additional charge on subscription.

Whilst vehicle hotspot is turned ON, all other Wi-Fi functionality, such as Wireless Apple CarPlay will be disconnected.

More details are available on the NissanConnect Services app.
