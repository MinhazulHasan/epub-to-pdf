Electric Shift Control system warning light

[SHIFT CONTROL, EXCLAMATION MARK, GEAR]

Illuminates when a malfunction occurs in the Electric Shift Control system.

When the master warning light illuminates, the chime sounds and the following message is displayed in the vehicle information display: [Apply parking brake].

When the power switch is placed in the OFF position, the chime sounds continuously. Ensure the parking brake is applied.

If the power switch cannot be placed in the OFF position, apply the parking brake and then place it in the OFF position.

Contact a NISSAN certified electric vehicle dealer for support.
