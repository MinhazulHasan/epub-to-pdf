PUSH BUTTON POWER SWITCH

[PUSH BUTTON POWER SWITCH]

When the power switch is pushed without depressing the brake pedal, the power switch position will change as follows:

  • Once to change to ON.
  • Two times to change to OFF.

When the power switch is pushed with the brake pedal depressed, the power switch position will change to the READY to Drive position.

The power switch will automatically return to the LOCK position when any door is either opened or closed with the switch in the OFF position.

Emergency shut-off

To shut off the electric vehicle system in an emergency situation while driving or when the Intelligent Key battery is discharged, perform the following procedure:

  • Push the power switch 3 times within 1.5 seconds
  • Push and hold the power switch for more than 2 seconds
