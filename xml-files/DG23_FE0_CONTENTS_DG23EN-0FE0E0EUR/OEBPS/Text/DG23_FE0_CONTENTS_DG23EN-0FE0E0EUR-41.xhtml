NAVIGATION LIVE TRAFFIC

[INFOTAINMENT & MULTIMEDIA, #8A78B6, #DBD5EC]

To activate Navigation Live Traffic information (Premium Traffic), follow the steps below:

1

Touch the [Navigation] icon from the [All Settings] menu.

2

Touch [Map Settings].

3

Touch [Traffic Info Settings].

4

Make sure [Use Premium Traffic] is set to [ON].

5

You can confirm the Navigation live traffic is on via the [TOMTOM TRAFFIC] icon on the bottom corner of the map screen.

Note:
  • If words [TOMTOM TRAFFIC] are not present, Premium Traffic is either off or vehicle has lost connection.
  • Requires a subscription to NissanConnect Services.
