REAR CROSS TRAFFIC ALERT

[DRIVING AIDS, #1CACE4, #BCE4FA]

The Rear Cross Traffic Alert (RCTA) system will assist you when reversing out of a parking space. When the vehicle is in reverse, the system is designed to detect other vehicles approaching from the right or left of the vehicle.

Use the vehicle information display to turn ON/OFF the IDA system.

[Settings] -> [Driver Assistance] -> [Cross Traffic Alert]

The RCTA system will be set to ON by default, the staOteff is remembered when the vehicle is restarted.
