PARKING BRAKE

[GETTING STARTED, #F18626, #FDDDC0]

To release/apply automatically:

Release:

The parking brake will automatically release as you drive away.

Apply:

Applies automatically when ignition is switched off and auto-hold is applied.

To release/apply manually:

Release:

With the ignition in the ON position, depress the brake pedal and push the switch down.

Apply:

Pull the parking brake switch up to apply.

Confirm indicator lights status in the vehicle information display.

Note:
  • Make sure the parking brake is applied before leaving the vehicle.
  • Before driving, be sure that the brake warning light has turned off.
