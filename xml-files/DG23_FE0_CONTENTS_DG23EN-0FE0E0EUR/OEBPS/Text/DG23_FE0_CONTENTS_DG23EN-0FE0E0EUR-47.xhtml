CRUISE CONTROL

[DRIVING AIDS, #1CACE4, #BCE4FA]

Conventional Cruise Control will maintain a desired speed but will not manage the distance from the vehicle in front. Steering Assist is not available for Conventional Cruise Control.

6

Set speed / Decrease speed

1

Press and hold the indicated switch above to activate the Conventional Cruise Control mode.

2
