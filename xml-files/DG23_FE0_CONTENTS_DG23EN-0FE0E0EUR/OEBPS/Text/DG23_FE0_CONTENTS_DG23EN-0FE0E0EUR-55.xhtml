BLIND SPOT WARNING (BSW)

[DRIVING AIDS, #1CACE4, #BCE4FA]

The Blind Spot Indicator light on the door mirror will illuminate if a vehicle is detected within the blind spot zone.

Blind Spot Warning light illuminates on the side of detected vehicle and in the vehicle information display.

Use the vehicle information display to turn ON/OFF the BSW system.

[Settings] -> [Driver Assistance] -> [Blind Spot] -> [Warning]

The BSW system will be set to ON by default, the stOaNte is remembered when the vehicle is restarted.
