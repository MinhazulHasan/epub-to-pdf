ELECTRONIC PARKING BRAKE SWITCH

[AUTO HOLD, P-SWITCH, ELECTRIC PARKING BRAKE]

The electronic parking brake can be applied or released by operating the parking brake switch.

To apply

Pull the electronic parking brake switch. The indicator light on the switch will illuminate.

To release

With the power switch in the ON position, depress the brake pedal and push the switch. The indicator light on the switch will turn off.

Before driving, check that the electronic parking brake indicator light goes out.
