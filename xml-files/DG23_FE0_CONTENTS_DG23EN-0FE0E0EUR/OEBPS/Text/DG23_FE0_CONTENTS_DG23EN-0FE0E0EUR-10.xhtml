DOORS - UNLOCKING AND LOCKING

[GETTING STARTED, #F18626, #FDDDC0]


1

Locks doors and tailgate

2

Unlocks doors and tailgate

3

Opens and closes tailgate

When the Intelligent Key is within the range of operation, the door locks can be locked or unlocked by touching the capacitive unlock sensor. To unlock, hold handle and touch the outer capacitive sensor. To lock, only touch outer capacitive sensor.

Emergency key

The emergency key can be used to unlock the driver's door in emergency situations.
